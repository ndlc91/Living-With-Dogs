Living with Dogs

The Pros

  • Lots of love
  • Always have a friend around
  • People will love him/her and want to talk to you
  • Something to cuddle with at night

The Cons

  1. Hair everywhere
  2. Disobedient behavior
  3. Hogs the bed
  4. Sometimes pees in the hall

In spite of the cons, there is no greater joy than owning a dog.
I think that the best decision we ever made was to bring home our little bear.
